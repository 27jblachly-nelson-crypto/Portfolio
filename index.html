a brief introduction

when i was a toddler the only thing that interested me was whales. im not sure why and it doesnt really matter here besides the fact it showed i loved learning. every day i would learn something new about a whale and play with my little schleich whales and could never stop talking about them. after whales came trains, flowers, and books but that did not matter because the only thing that mattered for a tiny toddler was learning. many years later that admiration for learning persists, studying languages, history, and sports. my learning could never be confined to a textbook or a computer, and i learn new skills or things to do just for the sake of it, often i even hate it and stop doing it, but when you find those things you love learning, it clicks with you.
